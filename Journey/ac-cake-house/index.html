Journey / AC Cake House

AC Cake House (M) Sdn Bhd

A multi-stage journey from frontline retail exposure to operational coordination and leadership responsibility.

Node 01 — First Step Into the Working World (2017)

My professional journey started in the retail and food service industry. Working as a cashier and customer service staff exposed me to real business operations from day one — handling transactions, supporting customers, and learning how service quality impacts customer experience.

Key Skills: Customer Service • POS Operations • Retail Workflow • Communication

Node 03 — Learning Business From the Ground Up (2018–2021)

Over the years, I progressed through multiple operational roles including baker, cake decorator, and customer service specialist. Working across different departments gave me hands-on exposure to production, service delivery, and quality control.

This experience allowed me to understand how different functions come together to support a successful business operation.

Key Skills: Team Collaboration • Operational Awareness • Product Quality Control • Time Management

Node 04 — Moving Into Operations Management (2020–2021)

As my experience grew, I transitioned into an operations role where I supported outlet coordination, inventory monitoring, and internal reporting. This was my first exposure to the decision-making side of business operations.

Key Skills: Operational Coordination • Inventory Monitoring • Business Reporting • Organisational Management

Node 05 — Leading Teams & Driving Performance (2021–2023)

I later stepped into a leadership role managing outlet operations and supervising teams of up to 10–15 staff members. My responsibilities included staff training, operational planning, performance monitoring, and improving customer experience.

This role strengthened my leadership ability and taught me how to make decisions using real operational data.

Highlights: Improved staff efficiency through structured training • Enhanced customer satisfaction through service improvements • Implemented marketing campaigns to increase sales • Reduced operational waste through better inventory management

Skills Developed: Leadership • Operations Strategy • Data-Informed Decision Making • Performance Monitoring
