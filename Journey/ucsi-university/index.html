Learning Direction

Building a stronger business foundation

While gaining practical work experience, I pursued a Diploma in Management to strengthen my understanding of how organisations operate beyond daily execution. This phase introduced formal business thinking and helped connect real work experience to larger organisational structure.

The programme covered areas such as business management, marketing, economics, financial accounting, supply chain and logistics, business law, risk management, and entrepreneurship. These subjects provided a clearer view of how decisions are made across functions and how different parts of a business contribute to performance.

This stage strengthened my ability to think more strategically, analyse problems from a business perspective, and communicate with better structure and clarity.
