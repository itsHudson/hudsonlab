Global Support Experience

Learning to operate in a fast digital service system

In 2023, I joined Webhelp Malaysia as a Customer Service Executive supporting Taiwan Foodpanda users. This role exposed me to a more fast-paced and digitally driven customer support environment with greater volume, responsiveness, and service expectations.

Working in this setting improved my ability to manage more complex customer issues, maintain service standards under pressure, and follow structured escalation and SLA processes. It also strengthened communication in a cross-cultural service environment.

This phase added a different kind of discipline to my journey — one built around speed, clarity, customer empathy, and analytical problem solving inside a large service system.
